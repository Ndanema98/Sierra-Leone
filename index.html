Sierra Leone

The Republic of Sierra Leone, informally Salone, is a country on the southwest coast of West Africa. Sierra Leone has a tropical climate with a diverse environment ranging from savanna to rainforests.

Key Facts

Languages

English and Krio are used commonly throughout the country. A majority of the population either speak one of these two languages.

Independance

Sierra Leone gained their independace from Britain on the 27th April 1961. After being in the commonwealth for 16 years.

Capital City

Freetown is the capital city of Sierra Leone. The city was founded by a British Naval Lieutenant and was populated by freed American slaves from Nova Scotia, hence the name.

Sierra Leone's historical timeline

1808

The capital city freetown becomes a crown colony.

1961

Sierra Leone gain independance.

1991

The Sierra Leone civil war started. The RUF captured towns on the border of liberia.

1997

Sierra Leone was suspended by the UN security council.

1999

Parts of freetown seized from Ecomg by rebels. 5,000 were left dead and the city was devastated.

2002

War declared over. The UN completed a disarnment off over 45,000 fighters.

2014

Sierra Leone declares a state of emergency to tackle the Ebola outbreak which had already killed 700.

2016

Sierra Leone was declared Ebola free by The World Health Organisation.
